App developed for daily and weekly Duty Charts for Government Railway Police
Neelesh Ranjan Jha, Naman Agarwal, Abhishek Soni had received words of appreciation by Uttar Pradesh Police.

Neelesh Ranjan Jha, Naman Agarwal, Abhishek Soni had received words of appreciation for the “App developed for daily and weekly Duty Charts for Government Railway Police” at Uttar Pradesh. They have been commended by the Superintendent of Police, GRP, Prayagraj, UP for an app developed by them. This app generated daily and weekly Duty Charts for the Government Railway Police, thus helping the SP Office in maintaining transparency and impartiality, while reducing the time taken to do so manually from 2 hours to 2 minutes. Hats off to their noble contribution to society!
